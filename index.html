Flores & Corvo Sailing Adventure Trips

Welcome to Sailboat Tours around the Ocidental Group of azores! We offer amazing tours that will let you explore the beauty of the islands and experience nature in a unique way. Our sailboat tours will give you access to beautiful bays, the opportunity to explore marine life, and a chance to help the environment. Our tours are designed to give you an unforgettable experience and plenty of time to take in the sights and sounds of the islands. You’ll be able to take in a variety of marine life. With our tours, you’ll be able to experience the beauty of the islands in a way that you simply can’t get from land. We also have an amazing deal for those who want to help out the environment. If you participate in a 1-2 hour coastal cleanup of plastic, you’ll be eligible for a discount on your tour. This is a great way to give back to the environment and make a difference. So come and join us for a sailboat tour around the Ocidental Group of azores. You won’t regret it!
